Launching MashaRket’s Website
and Registration Platform

28th to 30th November, 2023

Kigali Conference and
Exhibition Village

The Mashariki African Film Festival (MAAFF) and Media Pros Africa (MPA) are proud to announce the unveiling of the MashaRket - Kigali International Content Market official website and registration portal, a revolutionary and groundbreaking platform set to be the convergence point for global media stakeholders to network, exchange insights and transact.

Building upon the success of eight editions of the Mashariki African Film Festival (MAAFF) and one of Africa's top Distribution Network, Media Pros Africa (MPA), this partnership opens up opportunities of growth particularly for African filmmakers and content creators. Here are the key Features and Benefits of the MashaRket Website:

  • Seamless and user-friendly delegate registration: The platform's user-friendly forms facilitate the registration of different delegates, capturing key information about their organizations and content requirements. This will enable delegates to pre-book meetings with relevant companies, fostering fruitful B2B and B2C engagements throughout the content market. A list of confirmed delegates will be shared with delegates weekly.
  • Multiple Payment Platforms: Our platform offers easy, seamless, and secure payment options through various payment models, accommodating mobile money and online payment methods.
  • Travel Guide: International delegates can book their travel through our portal. We are collaborating with a top travel company that will provide our guests with personalized itinerary for their stay in Rwanda.
  • Efficient Meeting Pre-Booking: The platform's calendar feature empowers delegates to prebook meetings with other attendees, reserve stand spaces, and schedule attendance at various workshops and masterclasses within the content market

Mr. Tresor Senga, CEO of MashaRket. “The unveiling of the MashaRket website and registration portal stands as a true testament to the dedication and unwavering efforts of our team. This is a significant milestone after months of meticulous planning, says Tresor Senga, CEO Mashariki International Film Festival. He adds, “Our team shines through, and we are genuinely excited to see the launch of a user-friendly platform that connects producers, filmmakers, and professionals from across the globe. This initiative is a resounding testament to the potential of collaboration and innovation within Africa's film, media, and broadcast industry. We celebrate this achievement and eagerly anticipate the positive impact it will have on the sector's growth and advancement

Join the MashaRket Community today by simply registering via www.masharket.com The MashaRket website and social media platforms will be continuously updated you with fresh content, insightful articles, and expert interviews, providing users with the latest trends, best practices, and valuable insights to stay ahead in the dynamic media landscape.

MashaRket - Kigali International Content Market: Hosted under the auspices of the highly acclaimed nineth edition of the Mashariki African Film Festival is a key industry platform for both Francophone and Anglophone Africa. All delegates including content buyers, sellers, producers, and film students are welcome to register via our newly launched website.

MashaRket is the first content market hosted by MAAFF. The content market will create opportunities for sales, networking, and long-term relationships between local and international industry stakeholders. The 3-day content market taking place from 28 - 30 November 2023 at the Kigali Conference and Exhibition Village (KCEV) will bring together established and fastdeveloping creative industries, making content trading smoother and smarter for both sellers and buyers. Sellers can offer finished programs, adaptation rights and projects in development. Buyers represent broadcasters, streaming platforms, mobile operators, and producers among others.
